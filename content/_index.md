# Welcome to the HUBOT Chair Website

The HUBOT Chair (Human-Centered Programming by Demonstration for Collaborative Industrial Robotics) is an innovative, interdisciplinary research project supported by the MIAI Cluster IA. 

---

## The Grand Challenge
In the context of Industry 4.0, reprogramming industrial robots remains a costly and complex task requiring expert intervention. Traditional programming approaches fail to provide the flexibility needed for modern production environments characterized by high variability and customization.

HUBOT bridges this technical bottleneck by empowering non-expert operators to teach robots complex tasks through intuitive kinesthetic interaction (guiding the robot by hand). This transforms the robot from a rigid machine into a natural, collaborative instrument that supports the worker.
