# Open PhD Positions — MIAI Hubot Chair

The **MIAI Hubot Chair**, in collaboration with the **Laboratoire d'informatique de Grenoble (LIG)** (Université Grenoble Alpes) and the **Institut Pascal** (Université Clermont Auvergne / CNRS), is looking for talented and motivated candidates to fill two fully funded PhD positions starting in **September 2026**. Both positions are secured for a duration of 36 months by the Chair.

---

### 1. PhD Position: Neuro-Symbolic Learning of Planning Domains
* **Framework:** Funded by the MIAI Hubot Chair
* **Topic:** Applied to Programming by Demonstration for Collaborative Robotics
* **Hosting Labs:** LIG (Marvin team) & Institut Pascal
* **Supervisor:** Damien Pellier
* **Key Skills:** Python, PyTorch / TensorFlow, PDDL, Formal Logic
* [👉 Read the detailed job description (PDF)](upload/PhD_Neuro-Symbolic_Learning_of_Planning_Domains.pdf)

### 2. PhD Position: Robust HTN Planning Under Uncertainty
* **Framework:** Funded by the MIAI Hubot Chair
* **Topic:** Extension of HTN formalisms based on an interval representation of uncertainties
* **Hosting Labs:** LIG (Marvin team) & Institut Pascal
* **Supervisor:** Damien Pellier
* **Key Skills:** Rust / C++, Automated Planning, Constraint Programming or Optimization
* [👉 Read the detailed job description (PDF)](upload/PhD_Position_Robust_HTN_Planning_Under_Uncertainty.pdf)

### Application Procedures
Interested candidates must send an application package including a CV, a cover letter, Master's transcripts (M1/M2), and contact details for at least one academic reference to the PhD supervisor**.

* **Application Deadline:** Applications are reviewed on a rolling basis until the positions are filled.
